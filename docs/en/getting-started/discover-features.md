# Discover Features

Kamerplanter has many features -- but you don't need to learn them all at once. Depending on your experience level, Kamerplanter shows or hides features so you don't feel overwhelmed.

---

## For Beginners

These features are visible right away and are more than enough to get started:

### Manage Plants

Create new plants, edit them, or remove them. Each plant has a species (e.g. "Basil") and is assigned to a location (e.g. "Kitchen window").

**Learn more:** [Plant Master Data](../user-guide/plant-management.md)

### Set Up Locations

Describe where your plants are. Locations can be nested -- e.g. "Apartment > Living Room > South Windowsill".

**Learn more:** [Locations & Substrates](../user-guide/locations-substrates.md)

### Complete Tasks

Kamerplanter automatically creates tasks like "Water", "Repot", or "Fertilize". You see what's due today and check off completed tasks.

**Learn more:** [Tasks](../user-guide/tasks.md)

### Care Reminders

Automatic reminders for watering, fertilizing, and other care steps. Kamerplanter adjusts intervals to the season and your plant's needs.

**Learn more:** [Care Reminders](../user-guide/care-reminders.md)

### Dashboard

Your personal cockpit: open tasks, plant status, and care reminders at a glance.

**Learn more:** [Dashboard](../user-guide/dashboard.md)

---

## For Intermediate Users

When you're ready, switch your experience level to **Intermediate**. These features become available:

### Fertilization & Nutrients

Create nutrient plans, calculate nutrient solutions, and track EC values. Kamerplanter knows common fertilizer products and calculates mixing ratios.

**Learn more:** [Fertilization Logic](../user-guide/fertilization.md)

### Watering Log

Document each watering event with volume, EC, and pH. Kamerplanter shows you trends and warns about deviations.

**Learn more:** [Watering Log](../user-guide/watering-log.md)

### Tank Management

Manage nutrient solution tanks with fill level, EC, and pH. Ideal when you're feeding multiple plants from the same solution.

**Learn more:** [Tank Management](../user-guide/tanks.md)

### Calendar

All dates and tasks in a calendar view. Exportable as an iCal feed for your calendar app.

**Learn more:** [Calendar](../user-guide/calendar.md)

### Growth Phases

Track which phase your plants are in (germination, vegetative, flowering, harvest) and change phases manually or automatically.

**Learn more:** [Growth Phases](../user-guide/growth-phases.md)

---

## For Experts

At the **Expert** level, all features are available:

### Pest Management (IPM)

Integrated pest management with pest/disease tracking, treatment logs, waiting periods, and resistance management.

**Learn more:** [Pest Management (IPM)](../user-guide/pest-management.md)

### Planting Runs

Group plants into runs -- ideal for seasonal growing cycles or multiple tents.

**Learn more:** [Planting Runs](../user-guide/planting-runs.md)

### Harvest

Record harvest indicators, document yields, and assess quality. With automatic waiting period checks.

**Learn more:** [Harvest](../user-guide/harvest.md)

### Sensors

Connect sensors -- manually, via Home Assistant, or via MQTT. Kamerplanter displays VPD, temperature, humidity, and more.

**Learn more:** [Sensors](../user-guide/sensors.md)

### Actuator Control

Control fans, lights, and pumps via Home Assistant or MQTT. Rule-based control with hysteresis and safety limits.

**Learn more:** [Actuator Control](../user-guide/actuator-control.md)

### Propagation

Track cuttings, sowings, and crosses. Kamerplanter maintains a genetic family tree across generations.

**Learn more:** [Propagation](../user-guide/propagation.md)

### AI Assistant

Ask questions about plant care and get answers based on the integrated knowledge base.

**Learn more:** [AI Assistant](../user-guide/ai-assistant.md)

---

## Change your experience level

You can switch levels at any time:

1. Click your **profile picture** (top right)
2. Select **Account Settings**
3. Go to the **Experience Level** tab
4. Choose your desired level

Don't worry: switching levels never deletes data. Kamerplanter only shows or hides UI elements.
