# Your First Plant

This guide walks you step by step from opening Kamerplanter for the first time to having your first plant -- complete with a watering schedule and tasks.

---

## 1. Start the Onboarding Wizard

When you open Kamerplanter for the first time, the **Onboarding Wizard** greets you. It guides you through setup in five short steps.

!!! info "Already completed the wizard?"
    You can restart the wizard at any time: Go to **Account Settings** and click **Restart Onboarding Wizard**.

---

## 2. Choose your experience level

In the first step, choose how much experience you have with plant care:

| Level | What you see |
|-------|-------------|
| **Beginner** | Only the core features: plants, locations, tasks, watering |
| **Intermediate** | Plus fertilization, tanks, sensors, and calendar |
| **Expert** | All features -- including IPM, VPD, EC tracking, and propagation |

You can change the level **at any time** without losing data. Kamerplanter only hides UI elements -- your data remains fully intact.

---

## 3. Describe your environment

Briefly describe where your plants are. For example:

- "Kitchen window, south-facing"
- "Balcony, partial shade"
- "Grow tent 120x120"

Kamerplanter uses this info to suggest suitable starter kits.

---

## 4. Choose a Starter Kit

A starter kit is a pre-configured package with plant species, locations, and matching settings. Just pick the one that fits you best:

| Starter Kit | What you get |
|-------------|-------------|
| Windowsill Herbs | Basil, parsley, chives -- perfect for getting started |
| Houseplant Starter | Monstera, pothos, peace lily -- the most popular houseplants |
| Pet-Friendly Houseplants | Only non-toxic plants for homes with pets |
| Balcony Tomatoes | Tomato, pepper -- balcony vegetables for beginners |
| Succulents & Cacti | Low-maintenance succulents |
| Mediterranean Herbs | Rosemary, thyme, oregano |
| Balcony Chillies | Various chilli varieties for the balcony |
| Vegetable Bed | Classic vegetable bed with companion planting |
| Indoor Grow Tent | Controlled indoor growing with phase management |

!!! tip "No matching kit?"
    No problem -- you can always add your own plants after the wizard. The kits are just a quick start.

---

## 5. Set plant counts & favorites

In the next step, you see the plants from your chosen kit. Here you can:

- Adjust the **count** per plant type (e.g. 3 basil instead of 1)
- Mark plants as **favorites** -- these appear at the top of your dashboard

---

## 6. Review and finish

Review the summary and click **Finish**. Kamerplanter now automatically creates:

- Your **plants** with matching growth phases
- A **location** based on your environment description
- Initial **tasks** (e.g. "Water basil", "Check location")

---

## 7. Your Dashboard

After the wizard, you land on the **Dashboard**. Here you see at a glance:

- **Upcoming tasks** -- what needs to be done today
- **Your plants** -- with current status and phase
- **Care reminders** -- when the next watering round is due
