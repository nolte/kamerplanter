# Nutrient Mixing

A correctly mixed nutrient solution is essential for optimal plant growth. Kamerplanter automatically calculates EC budgets and validates the mixing order.

!!! danger "Mixing Order"
    Always add CalMag **first** to water before adding other fertilizers — especially before sulfates. Wrong order causes precipitation and an ineffective nutrient solution.
